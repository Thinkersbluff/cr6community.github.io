## Where's "the beef?"
If you found this site but were looking for the actual Community firmware, you will find that in the [Releases section of the CR6 Community Firmware Repository](https://github.com/CR6Community/Marlin/releases).

If you are actually looking for the re-factored Touchscreen Firmware, that is in [the releases section of its own repository](https://github.com/CR6Community/CR-6-touchscreen/releases)

CAUTION:  The above links take you to the Releases section, which contains ALL of the releases, not just the latest.
If you just scroll to the bottom of those pages and start downloading things, YOU will NOT BE HAPPY!

INSTEAD, click on the title of the release at the TOP of the Releases page, first, to go to the latest, then read the README, then you will know what to download.
