# A "Ticking Time-Bomb" of Sorts...
One of the disappointing CR6 printer design decisions made by Creality was the pair of ribbon cable connectors unique to the motherboard.  Those two connectors impose a (challenging but not insurmountable) barrier between this printer and all other motherboard options.  

What makes this barrier all the more disappointing and frustrating in 2026, is that Creality has apparently also now quietly stopped manufacturing and shipping new motherboards for the CR6 printer series.  Amazon listings for "compatible" boards show Chinese manufacturer's names like:
 - DAIHAOUU (model DAIHAOUU105, ASIN B0F4KCV51G)
 - AHGDFJLH (ASIN B0G44S5TNT)
at prices more than twice that of a BTT SKR mini E3 V3 motherboard.

For a brief period in 2020/21, BigTreeTech sold an alternative board (BTT SKR CR6 v1.0), which was quite popular, but which also subsequently went out of production.

It looks very important to try to avoid damaging your existing motherboard, if you want to keep your CR6 printer going.
